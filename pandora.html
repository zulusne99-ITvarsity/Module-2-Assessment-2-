Pandora

Pandora designs and manufactures hand-finished, contemporary jewellery. Their mission is to offer woman around the world a universe of high quality, modern and genuine jewellery products at affordable prices thereby inspiring woman to express their individuality. Pandora jewellery is sold in more than 100 countries on six continents through around 7,800 points of sale, including more than 2,400 concept stores.The Pandora story is about a company with a distinctive brand and distinctive products that in just a few years has made the exceptional journey from a local Danish jeweler to a world-leading international jewelry company, with sales in more than 70 countries.
