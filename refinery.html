Refinery

Refinery is a contemporary fashion retailer catering to today’s inspired young adults.The Refinery brand is determined to break the perception that quality and affordability are mutually exclusive. In fact, authenticity, detail and quality are fundamental to every part of the Refinery business.Refinery eliminates the non-essential and excessive aspects of fashion to give customers a curated, compelling brand experience that feels accessible and affordable. Refinery stores offer a mix of casual men’s and ladies’ wear, as well as a select range of accessories and shoes. The brand has a reputation for complementing seasonal looks and is particularly well-recognised for its sweatshirts and t-shirts.Refinery is notable for its focus on, and passion for, detail. All Refinery styles and products have a tactile quality that feels good. The brand is proud of its uniquely collaborative, creative way of working, which features a strong focus on transparency and honesty. True value defines the heart of the Refinery brand, and passion is the ultimate driver of the business.
